About us

Aarya bhavan Restaurants have taken shape from our family legacy in the restaurant business which began half a century ago. Initially based in Tirunelveli, the group's first restaurant in Coimbatore was set up in 2014. A unique blend of traditional hospitality and modern professionalism sets our restaurants apart. At our restaurants, we focus on taste, service, ambience and tidiness to give our patrons an enjoyable dining experience.
